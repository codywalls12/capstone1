Graph Sonification

The design of the website is built with the core principles of accessibility and data visualization in mind. Our primary objective is to make it easier for users of all backgrounds to access and manipulate their data in a visually appealing manner. The user will be able to initiate their data visualization by uploading their file whether it be a spreadsheet, csv, or any other common data formats. Once the data is uploaded the platform will seamlessly convert the data into a visually appealing graph or your choice.play Our intuitive graph creation tools will allow users to select specific sections and customize the visual elements. Even after the graph is created, the user maintains complete control. They can easily edit the underlying data, update values, and even add new information. The platform will automatically keep the visual representation up to date as their data changes. For any user who requires an auditory experience, the website will offer sonification capabilities. The user can have their graph represented audibly, with data points and trends translated into sound providing a unique way to understand the information in the graph. This sonification feature will cater to those with visual impairments and will also add a new dimension to interpret data. When the user is satisfied with the graph representing their data, they can effortlessly export the data in various formats, including pdf. The export feature will allow the user to save their visualized data to their desired location making it easier to share their information into maybe reports, presentation and or other documents.
